NCFG Crusher

This is a tool used to check and of the 6 DSi WiFi configuration slots. NCFGCrusher can view hex dumps of each slot, as well as delete slots. WiFi slots 1-3 are listed as SLOT_1, SLOT_2, and SLOT_3, and WiFi slots 4-6 (advanced setup connection) are listed as SLOT_EX_1, SLOT_EX_2, and SLOT_EX_3.
