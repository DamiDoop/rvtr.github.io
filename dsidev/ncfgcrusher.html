NCFG Crusher

This is used to view hex dumps of and delete the DSi WiFi configuration slots. WiFi slots 1-3 are listed as SLOT_1, SLOT_2, and SLOT_3, and WiFi slots 4-6(advanced setup connection) are listed as SLOT_EX_1, SLOT_EX_2, and SLOT_EX_3.
