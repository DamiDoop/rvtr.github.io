--- layout: devinfo app: tcl_2 redirect_from: /dsidev/tcl-2.html --- tcl-2 converts HNI_0037.bmp and HNI_0040.bmp (found in romfs) to .jpg, then copies them to the photo region of NAND (also updates pit.bin accordingly). The copied images are valid and pass PhotoChecker's tests.

There were two other photos (boy.bmp and 001.bmp) of a young boy and a slice of cake found in nitrofs. These are not not used in the demo.

This app is just like the tcl-2 demo in the TWL SDK, but with different images.
